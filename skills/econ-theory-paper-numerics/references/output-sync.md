# Output Sync Checklist

## Before Editing

- Identify the generating module, not just the visible artifact.
- Check whether the request targets exploratory output, draft output, or paper output.

## During Work

- Prefer smoke or fast profiles first.
- Keep output directories separate by profile or run.
- Track which figures and tables the manuscript actually imports.

## Before Declaring Success

- Re-run the relevant numerical entrypoint.
- Re-run the smallest relevant test or smoke suite.
- Confirm the manuscript references the regenerated artifact, not an older export.
- Compile the manuscript only after numerical outputs are current.
