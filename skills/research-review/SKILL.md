# Research Review

## Overview

Front door for evaluating research work. This skill covers referee-style review, methodological critique, structured scoring, and adversarial quality checks for manuscripts, proposals, and empirical designs.

## Use This For

- paper reviews
- grant reviews
- methodological critique
- evidence-quality assessment
- structured scoring of scholarly work
- research QA before submission

## Do Not Use This For

- creating the prose itself: use `research-writing`
- searching for sources: use `research-discovery`
- reviewing code: use `code-review`
- reviewing slide decks: use `slide-review`

## Internal Routing

- referee-style reviews
- checklist-driven manuscript review
- adversarial QA
- scholarly scoring frameworks
- critical-thinking review passes

## Operating Rule

If the user wants a critique rather than a draft, start here.
